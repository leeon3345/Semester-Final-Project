Travel Tips

Tap any section to expand it.

Useful Apps

Grab, LINE, Google Maps, XE Currency, Agoda, AirVisual.

Emergency Contacts

Police: 191 • Ambulance: 1669 • Fire: 199

Cultural Etiquette

Remove shoes at temples/homes, avoid touching heads, dress modestly in religious sites.

Payment

Cash for street vendors; cards in malls. Keep small bills for taxis.

Where to Stay

Sukhumvit (nightlife), Siam (shopping), Old City (culture), Silom (business).

Basic Phrases

Hello: Sawadee ka/krap • Thank you: Khob khun ka/krap • How much?: Tao rai?

Transport

Use BTS/MRT for traffic-free travel; Grab for door-to-door; insist on meter in taxis.

Common Scams

Tuk-tuk gem-shop detours, fake 'temple closed' trick, unmetered taxis overcharging.
